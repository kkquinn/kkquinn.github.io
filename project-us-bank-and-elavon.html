Key Accomplishments at U.S. Bank and Elavon

  • U.S. Bank Website
  • Elavon Website

During my tenure at U.S. Bank and Elavon, I was involved in several key projects that significantly improved the functionality and security of the merchant dashboard.

This included the development of a 508a and a11y compliant website merchant dashboard, and working with penetration testers to identify and patch potential security vulnerabilities.

  • Developed a 508a and a11y compliant website merchant dashboard, ensuring the site was accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.
  • Built and maintained merchant administration dashboard for U.S. Bank (Elavon Inc) using Angular JS, Angular, and Dotnet Blazor, enhancing the functionality and user experience of the dashboard.
  • Worked with penetration testers to identify potential security vulnerabilities and patched them based on priority and severity levels, improving the security of the website.
